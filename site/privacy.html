Privacy Policy

Fold Book is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device using the iOS Application Support directory. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The app makes no network requests except those required by Apple's StoreKit to process optional Pro subscription purchases.

We do not use analytics, third-party trackers, or advertising SDKs.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it. You can also clear all data at any time from Settings → Delete All Data.
